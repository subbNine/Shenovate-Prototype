Shenovate is a non-profit organization founded in Nigeria.
That bridges the gender Gap In technology, by empowering
girls and young women between the ages of 9-35 years, with Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths(STEM) through training in coding, robotics design and confidence skills to solve real-world problem

About Us

Mission

We are on a mission to bridge the gender gap in technology by empowering women and girls in Nigeria with the knowledge, skills, and support needed to excel in STEM fields.

Vision

We envision a future where women and girls have equal access to opportunities, resources, and support in S.T.E.M., enabling them to become leaders, innovators, and changemaker

Founders' Thought

Innovation and progress come from diverse perspectives. By fostering an inclusive environment where women and girls can learn, grow, and lead, we aim to create a more equitable and dynamic tech community.
